## Overview
WhatsApp Status lets you share temporary updates that disappear after 24 hours. You can share photos, videos, text, voice, and music. Your status updates are end-to-end encrypted and visible only to the contacts you choose.

This guide explains how to update your WhatsApp status on Android devices using different status types.

### Types of Status

WhatsApp offers different types of status that allow you to share updates in multiple ways. 

You can post the status using the following types:

 - **Text** to share short messages.
 - **Photos** to share images.
 - **Videos** to share video clips.
 - **Voice** to share audio messages.
 - **Music** to share posts with music. 



## Updating Status on Android Devices

This section describes how to update your WhatsApp status using text, photos, videos, voice, and music on Android devices.

**Prerequisites**

Before you start, check the following requirements:

- **Enable permissions**: Allow access to the camera, microphone, and gallery.
- **Ensure an internet connection**: Use Wi-Fi or mobile data.
- **Update the application**: Install the latest version of WhatsApp.

**Status Privacy Options**

WhatsApp provides status privacy settings that allow you to control who can view your status updates. 

 To set the privacy option, perform the following steps:
  1. On your device, open **WhatsApp**.
  2. At the top of the screen, tap the **three-dot** icon.
  3. In the drop-down menu, select  **Settings**.
  4. In the **Settings** list, tap **Privacy**.
  5. In the **Privacy** options, select **Status**. 
   
In Status, choose one of the following options:


- **My contacts**: Share updates with all contacts.
- **My contacts except...**: Share updates with all contacts except those you choose.
- **Only share with...**: Share updates only with selected contacts.
 

### Using Text

To update your status with text, perform the following steps:

1.	On your device, open **WhatsApp**.
2.	At the bottom of the screen, tap **Updates**.
3.	On the **Updates** screen, tap the **Pencil** icon.
4.	On the **Text** screen, type your message. 
To format or edit your text, tap and hold the text to select, then choose the following options:
    - **Standard tools**: Tap **Translate**, **Cut**, **Copy**, or **Undo**.
    - **Format**: Tap the **three-dot** button to select
**Bold**, **Italic**, **Strikethrough**, **Monospace**, or **Share**.
5.	At the bottom left, tap **Status** to choose your contacts.
6.	At the bottom right, tap the **Send** icon to share your update.

You update a text status.

### Using Photo

To update your status with a photo, perform the following steps:

1.	On your device, open **WhatsApp**.
2.	At the bottom of the screen, tap **Updates**.
3.	On the **Updates** screen, tap the **Camera** icon or **Add status**.
4.	In your gallery, select a photo.
5.	At the top of the photo screen, use the following tools to customize your photo:

    - Tap ![Image](text.jpg) to adjust color, alignment, and background.

    - Tap ![Image](sticker.png) to add location, time, emojis, and stickers.

    - Tap ![Image](pencil.png) to draw.

    - Tap ![Image](music.png) to add a song.

    - Tap ![Image](crop.png) to rotate or resize your photo.

6.	Below the photo on the left, tap **Add a caption** to type a message.
7.	Below the photo on the right, tap the **@** icon to mention your contacts.
8.	At the bottom left, tap **Status** to choose your contacts.
9.	At the bottom right, tap the **Send** icon to share your update.

You update a photo status.

### Using Voice 

To update your status with voice, perform the following steps:

1.	On your device, open **WhatsApp**.
2.	At the bottom of the screen, tap **Updates**.
3.	On the **Updates** screen, tap the **Pencil** icon.
4.	In the **Text** screen, tap **Voice**.
5.	In the **Voice** screen, record your message:

    - **Record**: Tap and hold the **Microphone** icon.

    - **Stop**: Release the **Microphone** icon.

6.	In the **Voice** message area, manage your recording:

    - **Review**: Tap the **Play** icon to listen to your message.

    - **Remove**: Tap the **Delete** icon to discard your message.

7.	At the top of the screen, tap ![Image](background.png) to change the background colour.
8.	At the bottom left, tap **Status** to choose your contacts.
9.	At the bottom right, tap the **Send** icon to share your update.

You update a voice status.
